👋 About

I am a Senior AI Research Scientist and PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at Bar-Ilan University, advised by Prof. Zeev Zalevsky (Dean of Engineering). My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, nano-optics, neuroscience, and biomedicine — developing deep learning models for advanced biomedical imaging and non-invasive sensing via speckle pattern analysis.

In parallel, I serve as a Lead AI Researcher at Wix, working on AI systems and cybersecurity, and as a Faculty Lecturer at Tel-Aviv University and The Open University, teaching AI, operating systems, and security courses.

🔬 Research

My research focuses on photonic sensing and AI-driven biomedical imaging. Key areas include:

  • Non-invasive biomedical sensing — Remote detection of blood oxygen saturation, intraocular pressure, and physiological states using laser speckle patterns and deep learning.
  • Neural activity imaging — Visual cortex speckle imaging for shape recognition and monitoring brain responses to speech stimuli.
  • Photonic human identification — Deep learning of back-scattered laser speckle patterns for biometric identification.
  • Microsaccade tracking — Secondary speckle pattern analysis for precise eye micro-movement tracking.
  • Optical memory — Machine learning models for novel optical memory approaches.
  • AI for cybersecurity — Detection algorithms and ML-based threat modeling at Wix, including LLM security research.
